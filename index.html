Canada's Food Guide defines and promotes healthy eating for Canadians. It translates the science of nutrition and health into a healthy eating pattern. By following Canada's Food Guide, Canadians will be able to meet their nutrient needs and reduce their risk of obesity and chronic diseases such as type 2 diabetes, heart disease, certain types of cancer and osteoporosis.
